Rusty Nail

Stirred ? Whiskey

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Scotch
  • 1 oz Drambouie
  • Lemon or orange oil

Instructions

  1. Stir over ice
  2. Strain and pour over a big cube
  3. Express lemon or orange oil over the top and discard the swath

Glassware: Rocks Glass

Ice: Big cube

Garnish: Lemon oil or orange oil
